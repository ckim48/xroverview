About

GIST VR LAB

GIST VR LAB is a creative lab at Carnegie Mellon Universityâ€™s Center for Transformational Play (CTP). It is overseen by Professor Jessica Hammer who has won many awards including World Economic Forum Young Scientist award and Okawa Award for her work in extended reality (XR), game design, psychology and health technologies. She has also been supported by various organizations like the Heinz Foundation, Google, Amazon, NSF etc., which provide funds for different research projects.

The main goal of GIST VR Lab project is focused on developing AI combined with Virtual Reality technology applications towards promoting wellbeing specifically dealing with self-critical thoughts as well as stress management. In addition to that it strives to create an innovative environment where emotional aspects are taken care through sleep patterns among others suchlike immersive virtual reality environments can be utilized self-efficacy while enhancing self-esteem too.
